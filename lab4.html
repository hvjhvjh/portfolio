Kitten cafe

This app is designed for a family cat cafe, where visitors can enjoy not only delicious food, but also the company of furry animals. Users can learn more about the cats that live in the cafe, as well as get acquainted with the features of the establishment, its atmosphere, prices for services and other offers.
